Event Information

Join the DAO by buying a cup of coffee at one of our events. Once we're on a blockchain, a CT (Coffee Token) will be minted and distributed for every coffee purchased. Customers will receive a portion of the CT minted from their purchase.

Want to learn more? Join our weekly meetings (virtually on our discord or in-person) where coffee and crypto lovers can learn more about theCaféDAO and participate in ongoing projects. We hope to see you there!
